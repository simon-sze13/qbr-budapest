# Lab 3 — Validate & observe

## Objectives

- Demonstrate **visibility** after deploy: health, metrics, alerts, or audit—pick the angle your QBR emphasizes.
- Tie observations back to **operational outcomes** (MTTR, compliance, capacity).

## Steps

1. Open the **monitoring or insights** view for the workload from [Lab 2](lab-02-deploy.md).
2. Walk through **one alert, one metric, or one audit event** prepared by facilitators.
3. Optional: show **policy** or **backup** posture if included in your demo script.

!!! example "Choose one storyline"
    - **Reliability**: alerts + remediation path.  
    - **Compliance**: audit trail or policy violation / pass.  
    - **Efficiency**: resource view or cost signal (if enabled).

## Expected outcome

- Audience sees **evidence** that the platform is not only “deploy” but also **operate**.
