# 06 · Taikun Link

This module mirrors **[06 Taikun link](https://docs.taikun.cloud/CloudWorks/Exposing_Applications/06_Taikun_link/)** in the product documentation.

## What you will learn

- **Taikun Link** as a **fast path** to expose a service with a **dedicated hostname** and **HTTPS**.
- How Link reduces **manual DNS** and **certificate** steps for selected use cases.
- **Example scenarios** you can reuse in customer conversations (time-to-URL).

## Workshop exercise (facilitator-led)

1. In the UI, navigate to **Taikun Link** (or the menu entry your tenant uses) for the target **Service** / **workload**.
2. **Enable** or **create** a Link per product docs; capture the **issued hostname**.
3. Open the **HTTPS URL** and show **latency** from “no public URL” to “browser loads app” for the QBR story.
4. Contrast **Link** with **full custom Ingress + CertManager** (when each wins).
