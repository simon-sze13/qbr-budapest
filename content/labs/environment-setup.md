# Environment & project access

Complete this **before** the [Exposing applications](exposing-applications/index.md) modules.

## Objectives

- Confirm **sign-in** and **project** context.
- Identify the **cluster** and **namespace** (if applicable) used for the exposing-applications demos.

## Steps

1. Open the **Cloud Factory** URL provided for the workshop.
2. Sign in with your **workshop identity**.
3. Select the **workshop project** (name shared by facilitators).
4. Open the **Kubernetes** / **cluster** view your facilitator uses for DNS, Services, and Ingress demos.
